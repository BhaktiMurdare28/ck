City Center Complex — Mumbai

Our most ambitious undertaking to date, the City Center Complex is a mixed-use development spanning over 2.5 lakh square feet. The project features commercial spaces on the lower floors, premium residences above, and a rooftop community garden. Using our stage-tracking system, the client can monitor every phase — from foundation pouring to the final coat of paint — directly from their dashboard.

NH-48 Road Expansion

Infrastructure at scale requires a different level of coordination. The 18-kilometer highway expansion project demanded real-time material tracking across multiple depots, daily supervisor reports with geotagged photos, and weekly expense reconciliation. All of this was managed through a single dashboard, keeping every stakeholder informed.

Technology Meets Construction

What sets Core Konstruct apart is our unwavering commitment to using technology as a force multiplier. Every supervisor carries a connected device that feeds live data into our centralized system:

  • Real-time stage progression — Supervisors update stage completion via a simple slider, instantly reflected across admin and client dashboards via WebSocket connections.
  • GPS-tagged photo documentation — Daily site photos are uploaded and automatically categorized by construction phase.
  • Automated expense tracking — Labour, material, and miscellaneous costs flow directly from daily reports into project finance dashboards.
  • Attendance monitoring — Worker attendance is digitally recorded each morning, giving admins visibility into on-site workforce strength.
"The shift from paper-based reporting to Core Konstruct's digital platform cut our administrative overhead by 60% and gave our clients a level of transparency they'd never experienced before."

— Rajesh Patil, Operations Head
